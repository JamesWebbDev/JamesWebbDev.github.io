Dungeon Diner

A top-down dungeon crawler where you hunt and slay monsters, then take their parts to create mouth-watering dishes for your customers.

Start Date: August 2022
Engine: Unity Game Engine
Role: Sole Programmer / Designer
Project Length: 4 months

Learnt skills:
  • A complex combat system between players and mobs with unique AI.
  • Scaling prototyped systems appropriately throughout development.
  • Developed tools to help streamline production.
  • Managed and implemented user feedback from extensive play-testing.
Prototype Gameplay:
  • Proof of Concept Gameplay (Week 3)
  • Alpha Gameplay (Week 12)
  • Beta Gameplay (Week 16)
  • Final Gameplay (Week 18)
